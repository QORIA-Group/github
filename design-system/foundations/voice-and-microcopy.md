# Voice and Microcopy

QORWAY speaks like infrastructure, not like an assistant.

## Voice attributes

```text
Sovereign
Clear
Structured
Explainable
Controlled
Technical without being obscure
```

## Preferred language

Use:

```text
Decision validated.
Execution locked.
Causal lineage incomplete.
PolicyCore requires human oversight.
Tenant boundary verified.
Feedback signal received.
Trace reconstruction available.
```

Avoid:

```text
Great, your decision is ready.
I found a good solution for you.
Let me think about that.
The AI recommends this.
Magic optimization complete.
```

## Button labels

Use:

```text
Validate Decision
Inspect Causal Path
Open Audit Trail
Release to PulseFlow
Review Policy Constraints
Compare Expected vs Actual
Lock Execution
Request Second Signature
```

Avoid:

```text
Ask AI
Generate Magic
Boost
Optimize Everything
Make it smarter
```

## Causal explanation format

```text
Signal:
What changed.

Context:
Where the change matters.

Causal Path:
Why the change creates risk or opportunity.

Decision:
What action is proposed.

Constraint:
What PolicyCore requires.

Execution:
What PulseFlow can orchestrate.

Feedback:
What will be measured.
```
